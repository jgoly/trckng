Welcome to trckng

Your coaching diary, always in your pocket. Log sessions, track your squad, and build a coaching diary that travels with you anywhere in the world.

Two ways to log

Use Quick log for fast trackside entries — athletes, type, quality, done in seconds. Use + Session for full detail including notes, results and reflections.

👥
Add your athletes first

Head to the Athletes tab and add your squad before logging your first session. Their names will appear in every session form ready to select. All data is stored privately on this device.
