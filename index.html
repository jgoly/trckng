How it works
01
Open the app

No download needed. Open the coach or athlete link below in Safari on your phone. Tap share, then "Add to Home Screen" and it works like a native app — including offline.

02
Log your session

Full session log or quick log in three taps. Results, notes, quality score. Your data is saved automatically as you type.

03
Build your record

Every session adds to a diary you can look back on. The calendar shows your consistency and quality trends month by month.
